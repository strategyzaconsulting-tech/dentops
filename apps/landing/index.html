Features

Everything your practice needs

One platform replaces the spreadsheets, paper forms, and disconnected tools your team has outgrown.

⏱

Real-Time Time Clock

Staff clock in from the mobile app or a location QR code. See who's on the floor live, track breaks, and flag tardiness automatically.

🌴

PTO Management

Staff request time off from their phone. You approve or deny with one tap. Visual team calendar and blackout date blocking keeps coverage gaps out.

📋

Digital Onboarding

New hires complete I-9, W-4, direct deposit, and emergency contact forms in the app — with e-signature and automatic W-4 annual review reminders.

📅

Shift Scheduling

Build weekly schedules, post open shifts, and let staff claim available hours. Integrated with the time clock for instant attendance tracking.

📢

Staff Announcements

Push office news and policy updates directly to every staff member's phone. Unread badges ensure nothing gets missed.

💼

Benefits & Compliance

Manage benefit elections per employee, track equipment assignments, and stay ahead of federal requirements with automated annual review workflows.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

No IT department required. No training day needed.

1

Set up your practice

Walk through a 9-step wizard to add your practice info, logo, locations, doctors, and staff. The whole thing takes under 10 minutes.

2

Staff download the app

Each team member gets the Brisa mobile app. They can clock in, request PTO, complete onboarding forms, and view their schedule — all from their phone.

3

You manage from the web

The admin portal gives you a live view of who's in, pending approvals, onboarding progress, and scheduling — across all your locations.
